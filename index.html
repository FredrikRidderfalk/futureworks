Beat Making

We've got a wide selection of beats for licensing in a variety of styles and genres. Get in touch and request a beat reel or book a studio session if you already have an idea that you want to develop.

Mixing & Mastering

If you have one of your own productions or a production that you've licensed from another producer and need it mixing with your vocal then we can get your beat mixed and mastered.

Vocal Recording

When hip hop is all about the vocals they've gotta be just right and so we use a top quality vocal mic setup for that high fidelity sound.

Complete Song Services

We specialise in creating bass heavy music - hip hop, trap, grime, drill, dubstep, UK garage and drum & bass. We write and create beats, have a high quality vocal recording setup and will see to it that your tracks are mixed and mastered to professional standards.

Project Launching

Making the music is only part of the process, and well thought out strategy is essential when putting out music. We can support you to develop your own comprehensive release plan to ensure that you get the most out of your project and achieve what you set out to achieve.
